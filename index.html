Infrastructure Facilities

Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) the nodal agency of Government of Kerala for entrepreneurship development in Kerala has set up various Future Technologies Accelerators to support startups working in emerging technologies like Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality, Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things etc.

The XR accelerator program focuses on startups in Augmented/ Virtual/ Mixed reality (XR) and gaming sector. The program aims to remove the entry barriers in the XR system today by giving mentorship, business connects and by cutting down the Software/Hardware cost.

Unity

Unity is the creator of the world’s most widely used real-time 3D development platform, giving developers around the world the tools to create rich, interactive 2D, 3D, VR and AR experiences.

Games and experiences made with Unity have reached ~3 billion devices worldwide and were installed more than 28 billion times in the last 12 months. Unity’s renowned flexibility gives developers the power to target and optimize their creations for  25+ platforms
